What BookVoice is

BookVoice is an iOS app for organizing and enjoying books—reading, listening, and related features—with optional use of the camera to scan pages or capture cover images, as described in the app and our Privacy Policy.

Common troubleshooting

  • Update the app — Install the latest version from the App Store.
  • Restart the app — Close it from the app switcher and open it again.
  • Restart your device — Resolves many temporary audio, network, or permission issues.
  • Check permissions — In Settings → BookVoice, confirm microphone, speech, network, or other access the app needs.
  • Reinstall — If problems persist, delete the app and reinstall (you may lose local data; see whether the app syncs or backs up).
