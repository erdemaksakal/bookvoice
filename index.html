Privacy Policy

BookVoice · Last updated: April 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BookVoice (“the app”) handles information when you use our iOS application. We built BookVoice with privacy in mind: we do not operate our own account system in the app, and we do not collect personal data on servers we control for advertising or profiling.

1. Information we do not collect

We do not ask you to create an in-app account with us. We do not knowingly collect, store, or sell your name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, or reading history on our own servers.

2. Data stored on your device

By default, your library (saved books), reading positions, preferences, and related app data are stored locally on your device. You can delete books, clear data from within the app where supported, or remove all app data by uninstalling the application.

3. Optional iCloud sync

If you enable Sync library with iCloud in Settings, your books and saved reading positions may be stored in your personal iCloud account so they can sync across devices signed in with the same Apple ID. That processing is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy. We do not receive a separate copy of your iCloud data on our servers.

4. Camera

BookVoice may use the device camera to scan book pages and to take photos for book covers, as described in the system permission prompt. Image capture and scanning are used to support reading features on your device; we do not upload your camera images to servers we operate.

5. Network requests and third-party content

When you choose to download or open books from public sources (for example Project Gutenberg, Internet Archive, or similar distributors), the app connects to those services to retrieve book content and related assets. Those providers may process connection data under their own policies; we do not attach your name, Apple ID, or contact details to those requests from the app.

The app may also request publicly available book metadata (for example to improve titles or descriptions) from third-party APIs. Such requests are limited to book-related information and do not include your personal identity fields.

The app may fetch a small remote configuration file (for example to determine a minimum supported app version). That check is generic and is not used to identify you personally.

6. Purchases (BookVoice Pro)

If you purchase a subscription or other in-app product, payment and subscription status are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment card number. Apple provides us with information needed to validate entitlements (for example subscription state) according to Apple’s systems.

7. Analytics and advertising

BookVoice is not designed to show third-party advertising, and we do not integrate third-party advertising SDKs for behavioral tracking. We do not sell your personal information.

8. Children’s privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). If you believe a child has provided personal information in a way that concerns you, please contact us using the details below.

9. International users

Because data may be processed on your device and, if you enable it, through Apple’s iCloud and the App Store, information may be handled in accordance with Apple’s infrastructure and the policies linked above.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how BookVoice handles data, please contact us through the App Store listing for BookVoice (Support / developer contact) or your usual support channel for the app.

This page is provided for App Store and web disclosure requirements. The in-app Privacy Policy screen may contain additional or localized wording; both describe the same overall practices.
